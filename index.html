what make our coffe special

Welcome to *Mind Blowing*, the coffee shop where you can stimulate your mind and enjoy a cup of coffee. We are a math and accounting themed coffee shop that serves coffee in calculators and spreadsheets, as well as delicious snacks and pastries. Our coffee is made from high-quality and fresh coffee beans, and our snacks are baked daily with love and care. We also have a variety of math puzzles and books for you to use and enjoy, as well as a display of charts and graphs from local businesses. Whether you want to solve, calculate, learn, read, or just relax, *Mind Blowing* is the place for you. Come and join us today and discover the math of coffee!
